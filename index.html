IMPORTANT

POINTER LOCK:

If this is ON your cursor will disappear and the window will have control of your mouse. Press ESC to have free control of your mouse again.

FLY:

If this is ON you will not be locked on the ground plane, so while holding W or the UP arrow, you will move directly to the middle of the screen even if that's above or below the map!
